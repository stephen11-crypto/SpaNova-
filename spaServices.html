Spa Services at Paradise Resort

Indulge in ultimate relaxation with our exclusive spa treatments designed to rejuvenate your body and mind.

Massage Therapy

Relax and unwind with our range of rejuvenating massages.

  • Swedish Massage: A soothing massage with long strokes to relax the body.
  • Deep Tissue Massage: Targets deeper layers to relieve muscle tension and stress.
  • Hot Stone Massage: Heated stones used for deep relaxation and improved circulation.
  • Aromatherapy Massage: Uses essential oils to promote relaxation and emotional well-being.

Facial Treatments

Revitalize your skin with our hydrating and rejuvenating facials.

  • Tropical Fruit Facial: Brightens and hydrates skin with tropical fruit extracts.
  • Anti-Aging Facial: Reduces fine lines and wrinkles with collagen boosting treatments.
  • Hydrating Facial: Provides deep moisture to dry skin, leaving it soft and refreshed.

Body Treatments

Enhance your skin's radiance with our exfoliating and hydrating body treatments.

  • Paradise Scrub & Glow: A scrub using exotic ingredients to exfoliate and hydrate your body.
  • Aloe Vera Body Wrap: Soothes and cools your skin, perfect for after sun exposure.
  • Mango Butter Body Wrap: Nourishes and deeply hydrates skin, leaving it soft and glowing.

Nail Care

Treat your hands and feet to luxury with our manicure and pedicure services.

  • Paradise Manicure/Pedicure: Includes nail care, soak, massage, and polish.
  • Citrus Spa Manicure/Pedicure: A citrus-infused scrub and mask for smooth, hydrated skin.
  • Deluxe Spa Pedicure: A pampering treatment with exfoliation, massage, and moisturizing mask.

Wellness & Relaxation

Find inner peace and relaxation with our wellness-focused treatments.

  • Couples' Retreat: Enjoy side-by-side massages and private relaxation time.
  • Sunset Yoga & Meditation: Calm your mind and body with sunset yoga and guided meditation.
  • Healing Hot Springs Soak: Relax in natural mineral hot springs for a full-body rejuvenation.
